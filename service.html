Our Services

Shahbaz Nursery Garden offers a wide range of services to help you create and maintain beautiful, sustainable Shahbaz spaces.

Garden Design Consultation

Personalized consultations to plan your dream garden. We provide expert advice on plant selection, layout, and sustainable practices.

Plant Delivery & Installation

We deliver and install plants, soil, and garden accessories directly at your location, ensuring hassle-free setup and care.

Landscaping Services

From small backyards to large estates, our landscaping team designs and maintains stunning outdoor environments.

Workshops & Training

Join our hands-on workshops to learn about propagation, composting, indoor gardening, and more. Great for families and enthusiasts!

Corporate & Office Solutions

We provide plant décor, maintenance, and Shahbaz design for offices, restaurants, and corporate spaces.

Seasonal Plant Care

Our experts guide you on seasonal plant care, pest management, and fertilization to keep your plants thriving year-round.
